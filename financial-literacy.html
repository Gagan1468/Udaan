💡 What is Financial Literacy?

Financial literacy means understanding how money works – how you earn it, save it, invest it, and spend it. It’s about making smart choices so you can control your future, not let your finances control you.

📚 Why It Matters

  • Helps avoid debt traps 🕳️
  • Improves savings and budgeting skills 💼
  • Makes you investment-ready 📈
  • Builds strong financial independence 🔐

🚀 Basic Concepts You Should Know

  • Income vs Expenses: Know what comes in and what goes out.
  • Budgeting: Create monthly budgets to avoid overspending.
  • Saving & Emergency Funds: Always keep backup 💸
  • Investments: Let money grow (mutual funds, stocks, SIPs etc.)
  • Loans & Credit: Know the terms before taking one. Interest rate matters!
