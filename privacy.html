Privacy Policy

We at Prerna are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and store your personal information.

What Information We Collect

When you use Prerna, we may collect your name, email, phone number, and profile picture which you voluntarily provide while registering or editing your profile.

How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to personalize your experience within the app. Your data stays on your device (via localStorage) and is not shared with anyone.

Data Security

We store your data locally in the browser using localStorage. This means your data is private and not sent to any external server.

Third-party Services

We may use third-party services like EmailJS for sending messages. These services follow their own privacy practices.

Your Control

You can edit or delete your profile anytime using the Edit Profile feature. Clearing your browser data will remove all saved information.

Contact

If you have any questions regarding your privacy, feel free to contact us via the Counsellor form available in the app.
